About Quizzal

Welcome to Quizzal, an interactive learning platform created to make studying easier, smarter, and more enjoyable.

Quizzal helps students test their knowledge through quizzes from different subjects including Mathematics, Chemistry, Physics, Geography, and many more.

Our mission is to combine education and technology to give learners a simple way to practice, improve their skills, and prepare for exams anytime and anywhere.
